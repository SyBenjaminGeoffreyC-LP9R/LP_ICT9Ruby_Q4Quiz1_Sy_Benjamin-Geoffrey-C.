Great Tourist Spots in the Philippines

Boracay

  • Boracay is a island that prides itself of its white and fine sand; beautiful beach; balmy weather; and warm and crystalline waters.
  • One of the top places for relaxation.



Palawan

  • It is an island home to multiple tourist spots such as Puerto Princesa and El Nido.
  • One of their most popular attractions is easily the Underground River (Puerto Princesa), which has been named one of the New 7 Wonders of Nature.

Cebu

  • Popularly known as the "Queen City of the South."
  • Both Northern and Southern Cebu have something to offer to you! Northern Cebu is known for it's beaches while Southern Cebu is one of the best places in the country for chasing waterfalls.
